About me

I'm a first-year Ph.D. student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-SZ), supervised by Professor Zhizheng Wu. My research interest is to study Music from the perspective of Artificial Intelligence. In particular, my ongoing works focus on Singing Voice Conversion (SVC). To know more about SVC, you can read this tutorial.

Before CUHK-SZ, I received my master's degree (2019-2022) at the Institute of Computing Technology Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT, CAS), working on computational social science, especially on Fake News Detection and Fact Checking.
